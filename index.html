Phase 1: Kick-off

The journey begins with the **kick-off phase**, a foundational and **pivotal meeting** where we align the client and supplier project teams. Here, we don't just present the organizational chart and schedule; we establish a clear roadmap and shared vision to ensure everyone is perfectly in sync.

Phase 2: Simulation and Method Plan

The next phase is **simulation**, where we transform abstract ideas into a detailed, concrete plan. This is a deep dive into the specifics, where we determine the **exact weight of the raw material** and the precise **number of operations** required. The output is our **method plan**, the ultimate blueprint that charts our course forward.

CAD Design and Physical Model

With the **method plan approved**, we move from digital simulation to tangible creation. We initiate the project's construction in **CAD (Computer-Aided Design)**, meticulously crafting a precise digital model. From there, we transition to the physical world, constructing a styrofoam model that will be sent directly to the foundry for casting.

Phase 3: Machining and Quality

Following the physical modeling, we proceed with **2D and 3D machining** and component assembly. The third phase is a critical one, focused on **quality convergence** and the production of **validation batches**. We typically conduct between three and four batches to rigorously ensure consistency. Once validated, the tools are ready to ship. For international suppliers, we strategically manage logistics, with transport handled by sea.

Phase 4: Adaptation and Series Production

The fourth phase is dedicated to the **adaptation of the process** for the final production environment. Here, we run additional **convergence batches** to fine-tune every detail, ensuring a **seamless transition** from development to full-scale series production.

Phase 5: Approval and Series Production

Finally, we arrive at the culmination of our efforts: **final approval of the product and process**, which signals the start of **series production**. This is the moment where our conceptual designs are fully realized as high-quality, tangible components, ready for the market.
